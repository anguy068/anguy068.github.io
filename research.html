What I'm Working On
Thales - Project Arduino

I was selected by Thales compete in groups against other universities, including UC Irvine, and utilize an Arduino Uno board to create a Thales-inspired project and showcase it to judges. My group chose to create a Sleep Detection and Wake Up device. Using a pulse-sensor, EMG, and accelerometer to detect head movement, our device monitors sleep patterns and detects movement during sleep. The pulse-sensor will monitor heartrate and create graphs of those results.
UCI 2017 MedApp Jam - Meddy Buddy

Currently developing an iOS application for the annual UCI 2017 MedApp Jam competition. My group is creating the Meddy Buddy app which will be used to help patients in hospital or in-home care settings daling with lonliness and provide a coping mechanism using our augmented reality avatar so the patient can communicate and converse with.
